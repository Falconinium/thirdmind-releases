## A collective brain for your circle.

Third Mind is a private desktop app for masterminds, study groups, and small teams who think together. Every link, video, podcast or article a member shares becomes part of a **living, shared brain** — summarized, classified, ranked for each of you, and searchable forever.

It is not a feed. It is not a chat tool. It is not a note-taking app. It is the curation layer of a serious collective.

---

## The idea

A mastermind works because minds align. But the knowledge that emerges from those conversations — the article one of you brought up, the podcast someone mentioned, the framework that explained everything — usually slips through the cracks.

Third Mind catches it.

- You share what you find.
- The AI summarizes it, classifies it, scores it for each member based on their domain and interests.
- Everyone sees what matters **to them**.
- Every Monday morning, a personalized digest lands in your inbox.

The circle, augmented.

## Headline features

### 🧠 The conversation channel
A private, Telegram-style channel where you share resources with context. Paste a link, write a note — the AI generates a structured embed (summary, key points, tags, relevance) right inside your message.

### ✨ AI summaries that respect your time
Every shared link gets a 3-sentence summary, the 3 key points that actually matter, and a tag from a curated vocabulary. Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash. Costs are negligible (~$1.50/month for a 15-person group).

### ⭐ Personalized relevance
The AI scores every resource for every member based on their domain, bio, and interests. Your channel surfaces the same shared timeline, but your stars are yours alone.

### 📂 Smart folders, auto-classified
Resources file themselves into folders the moment they're shared. No tagging chore. No quarterly clean-up.

### 🎮 Levels, streaks & a leaderboard
Each contribution earns XP. Streaks reward daily participation. A team leaderboard creates gentle, motivating accountability — Napoleon Hill's principle, in pixels.

### 🔍 Full-text search
Across every resource ever shared. Try finding that podcast someone mentioned three months ago — Third Mind has it indexed.

### 📨 The weekly digest
Every Monday at 8am: the 5 resources ranked highest **for you** that week, delivered as a clean email. Zero noise, signal only.

### 🔔 @Mentions with native notifications
Tag someone in a message; they get a real macOS or Windows notification, even with the app closed. The right people see the right thread at the right time.

### 🎨 Designed like a private members' club
Editorial dark mode, gold and violet accents, restrained typography. Built to feel like something you want to spend time in — not a corporate tool you tolerate.

## Get started

### 1. Install
Download the latest installer:
👉 **[github.com/Falconinium/thirdmind-releases/releases/tag/v0.3.1](https://github.com/Falconinium/thirdmind-releases/releases/tag/v0.3.1)**

- **macOS Apple Silicon** — `Third-Mind-x.y.z-arm64.dmg`
- **macOS Intel** — `Third-Mind-x.y.z.dmg`
- **Windows 10/11** — `Third-Mind-Setup-x.y.z.exe`

> First-time install? The release page includes a step-by-step guide for getting past macOS Gatekeeper and Windows SmartScreen.

### 2. Sign in
Open the app → click **Sign in** → type your email. A 6-digit code lands in your inbox. Type it. You're in.

### 3. Get invited (or create a team)
Third Mind is invitation-only. Ask an existing member for an invite link, or create your own team and invite the people who think with you.

## Tech stack
Built with [Nextron](https://github.com/saltyshiomix/nextron) (Next.js + Electron), Tailwind CSS, Supabase (Postgres + Auth + Realtime + Edge Functions), and Google Gemini for the AI pipeline. All client secrets live server-side in Edge Functions — the desktop binary holds nothing privileged.
